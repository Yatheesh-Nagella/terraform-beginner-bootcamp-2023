Ferrero Rocher Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g hazelnuts
  • 200g milk chocolate
  • 200g Nutella
  • 200g wafer cookies
  • 200g powdered sugar
  • 200g melted chocolate (for coating)
  • Whole hazelnuts for the center (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Spread hazelnuts on a baking sheet and roast for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Allow them to cool.
  3. Remove the skins from the hazelnuts by rubbing them with a kitchen towel.
  4. In a food processor, blend hazelnuts until they turn into hazelnut butter.
  5. Melt chocolate and mix it with Nutella, hazelnut butter, powdered sugar, and crushed wafer cookies.
  6. Take a small portion of the mixture, place a hazelnut in the center, and roll it into a ball.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining mixture and hazelnuts.
  8. Chill the hazelnut balls in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  9. Dip each chilled ball into melted chocolate to coat evenly.
  10. Place the coated balls on a tray lined with parchment paper and let them set in the refrigerator.
  11. Once set, your homemade Ferrero Rocher is ready to be enjoyed!

Enjoy your delicious homemade Ferrero Rocher!
